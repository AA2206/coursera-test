Three Football/Soccer Greats

Messi

Lional Messi is arguably the greatest individual talent football/soccer has every seen. On an individual level he has scored 821 goals, provided 362 assists, and won 8 Ballon d'Ors. On a team level he has notablly won 4 champions leagues, 12 league titles, and 1 World Cup.

Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo has had arguably the most impressive career for a soccer player. He has won every domestic trophy in 3 of the top 5 leagues and 5 champions league trophies. He has also helped portugal win their 2 only internatinal trophies (the Euros and Nations League). On an individual level he is the all time leading goal scorer with 876 goals and a 5 time Ballon d'Or winner.

Pele

Pele is no doubt one of the greatest legends of the game. His international career is second to none having won an 3 world cups while scoring 11 goals and providing 9 assists across the 3 wins. On an individual level he has scored 757 goals in official matches.
